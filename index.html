About

This website showcase cultural scenery across video games. I wanted to center my project around this topic because video games are a passion of mine and really wanted to make some of my academic projects about the things that I'm passionate about. I'm taking my time to learn about how to built a website from (nearly) scratch!

Visuals

Genshin Impact - China, Persona 5 Royal - modern day Japan, Assassin Creed Origin - ancient Egypt.
The locations presented in the game very closely mimic real life locations. Players are able to experience real places they haven't been before through video games that transcends both distance and time.
